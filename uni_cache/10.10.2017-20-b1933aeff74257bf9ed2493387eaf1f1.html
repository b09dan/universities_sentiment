\r\n \r\nWhat is your overall opinion of this school?\r\n\r\n

\r\n Harvard University is an outstanding institution with something incredible to offer for every one of its students. I came to college unsure of myself, my identity, and my path, but after just a single semester have found myself more sure of who I am than I ever could have imagined. The admissions committee is unbelievable. Everyone of my peers has something to teach me. I learn just as much from casual conversations with my friends as I do from my professors. I truly couldn't have dreamed of this experience. Whether I'm spending hours sitting in 'The Berg' munching on Marshmallow Mateys chatting with my friend Maisie, or grinding on an 'EC Pset' in the depths of Lamont Library, I always know that I've found my home at Harvard. \r\n

\r\n \r\nWhat is your overall opinion of this school?\r\n\r\n

\r\n Harvard University is a school that truly lives up to its name. As a student at the Extension School, the support of the faculty and academic staff is priceless, especially for the online courses. The professors are one of the best aspects of studying here, and they show their support every step of the way. I can only speak for the courses at the extension school which are modeled after those at the College or GSAS (depending on whether you're undergrad or grad), and I can say with confidence, that they deliver a world-class level of education which only a university like Harvard could provide. They also make every University affiliate feel at home. Whether you're Extension or College or any other school, you have access to the incredible Harvard libraries. That alone should motivate anyone who is truly interested in building a strong academic acumen to attend.\r\n
